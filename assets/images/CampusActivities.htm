What data gets entered here and why?

 
Individual sisters are expected to record their involvement in campus organizations other than Phi Sigma Sigma and any campus honors that they are bestowed using this web function. This function is also used to document personal service efforts performed outside of the chapter as well as leadership training seminars that one attends.
 
Chapter sister's accomplishments in all of these areas are measured as part of our Chapter Excellence program. Your chapter's CE score will be based on the data you enter here.
 
Each member of Phi Sigma Sigma is expected to participate in at least two campus organizations and be leaders in those organizations whenever possible.
 
A work schedule of 10 or more hours per week may be substituted for one campus organization. In such cases enter Work as the campus organization.
 
Please make separate entries for each campus organization that you are involved in, each service endeavour the you undertake, each training seminar that you attend, and each honor that you are bestowed.
 
Make a new entry when you assume a new leadership role within the organization. For example:
